    diamond About us

    Why Choose Us?

    - We aim to make sending money to Algeria as simple and convenient as possible. With our service, your funds are delivered on the same day without the need for back-and-forth communication or long waiting times. Simply log in, fill out a transfer form, and leave the rest to us. You can even request that we notify your recipient when the money is received, so you don’t have to worry about follow-ups.

    How It Works?

    - We’re able to deliver your money quickly because we maintain a reserve of Algerian dinars in Algeria. This allows us to send your recipient the money immediately, without waiting for your USD payment to clear. Essentially, we send the DZD first and sell your dollars afterward.

    How Do We Make Money?

    - Our revenue comes from exchanging your dollars for dinars at a slightly higher rate. By adding a small profit margin to the currency exchange, we ensure that you get fast and reliable service without any hidden fees.
